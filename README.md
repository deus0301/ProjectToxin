## üéÆ Overview

**Project TOXIN** is a third-person psychological thriller RPG set in a dystopian future where humanity has fled underground after toxic nanites consumed the surface. You play as a human-nanite hybrid created to reclaim the overworld‚Äîbut cursed to emit deadly toxins that kill any human you approach.

### Core Concept

- **You cannot interact with the living** - Only communicate with ghosts
- **Every choice matters** - Build morality by completing ghost quests or lose it by creating new spirits
- **Hybrid existence** - Balance your humanity against your nanite nature
- **Environmental storytelling** - Uncover the truth through corrupted beacons and ghost memories

## üéØ Key Features

### Ghost-Bound Interaction System
- Communicate exclusively with spirits who have unfinished business
- Complete quests to gain morality and unlock abilities
- Each ghost offers unique combat techniques and traversal powers

### Dynamic Morality System
- **High Morality Path**: Help ghosts find peace, send humans back underground safely
- **Low Morality Path**: Kill humans to create new ghosts, embrace your toxic nature
- Your choices determine the planet's future

### Nanite Absorption & Beacons
- **Passive Absorption**: Constantly collect stray nanites from the environment
- **Core Capacity Management**: Strategic resource management with maximum limits
- **Beacon Network**: 
  - Pure beacons: Recharge and upgrade abilities
  - Anti-Totems: Corrupted beacons that bypass human protections

### Combat System
- **Ghost-Linked Powers**: Gain combat techniques from spirits you've helped
- **Nanite Beasts**: Fight corrupted creatures and rogue hybrid prototypes
- **Boss Encounters**: Optional high-difficulty bosses drop rare anti-totems
- **Ability Trees**: Unlock both traversal and combat abilities (physical & nanite-based)

### World Design
- **Totems**: Protect humans from your toxic presence
- **Anti-Totems**: Allow you to bypass protections‚Äîbut at what cost?
- **Three Lab System**: MVP features a starting lab and two progression areas
- **Underground Society**: Explore the remnants of humanity's refuge

## üë• NPC Entities

### Humans
- **Quest Humans**: Unprotected individuals you can kill to create quest-giving ghosts (reduces morality)
- **Protected Humans**: Totem-wielding survivors you can kill or mercifully send underground

### Nanite Beasts
- **Normal Beasts**: Zombie-like creatures with alien structures (2/10 difficulty)
- **Nanite Bosses**: Stronger enemies that drop lore items and extra nanites (6/10 difficulty)
- **Optional Bosses**: Nearly impossible encounters that reward anti-totems (10/10 difficulty)

### Ghosts
- **Researcher Ghosts**: Progress the main storyline
- **World Ghosts**: Provide side quests and unique abilities
